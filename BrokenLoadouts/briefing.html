Description

Recon units have learned the location of several enemy forces getting ready for an attack. Command estimates that they could pose a major risk to our ground forces when the ground war begins. To prevent this, Command has demanded the immediate destruction of these units.

Tasks

  • Destroy the APC designated objective SWAP.

Remarks

  • Air defense units protecting objective SWAP can be ignored. Your primary target remains the APC.
  • Friendly units at objective SWAP can radio their coordinates.
  • Friendly units at objective SWAP can designate their position with laser. Laser code is 1688.
  • Friendly units at objective SWAP can mark their position with smoke.
